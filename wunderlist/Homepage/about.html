About WonderList 2.0

Wunderlist 2.0 was founded in 2019 when cofounder Randy Warrington was fed up with the lack of key features from traditional task managers, so he created his own. While still a student at Lambda School, Randy shared his app with his fellow cohort and the rest is history.

Today Wunderlist 2.0 employs three full time developers and is expanding to serve not just personal users but small businesses, enterprises, educators and students.

If you are interested in more information on how Wunderlist 2.0 can help your small business, enterprise, or classrom become more productive please submit a contact form in our contact us page.
